Cheerz

This is a simple app that can replace a "Cheer Box" at your next Pack Meeting or Court of Honor. At start up, the cheers are shuffled and displayed one at a time on the home screen. A complete list of cheers can be found on the Cheers screen.

Built with ionic

The beautiful, open source front-end framework for developing hybrid mobile apps with HTML5.
